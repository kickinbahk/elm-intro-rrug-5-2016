## What is Elm? Elm is a programming language that compiles to HTML5: HTML, CSS and JavaScript and used to build web apps.
### Similar to CoffeeScript or Typescript it Compiles to Javascript
### Elm doesn’t replicate or try to mend the intricacies of JavaScript.
### Elm is a language and ecosystem of its own, and it just happens to compile to JavaScript.

### General refactoring experience is: 1. Make changes, any changes. 2. The compiler tells what you missed. 3. Go to 1.
### Strong static types Find errors fast with readable compiler messages.
### No `null` or `undefined` Never miss a potential problem.
### Total immutability Focus on what you are writing right now without worrying about outer state.
### Purely functional Leads to decoupled and easily refactorable code.
### No runtime exceptions Gives incomparable reliability.
### Reactive by design FRP isn’t opt-in, it is baked right in the language.
